Introduction

The big, overarching question of this project on computers was about how computers have changed various aspects of the way we live. This is covering how different the way we work and play was and is now because of computers, as well as how these changes in computer technology occurred. I have been looking at the effects of this technology on mathematical calculations, how computer technology has changed the way music is consumed, and how scientific knowledge will transform computing to become even better and more powerful, making them more capable of performing tasks that are currently not possible.

The main reason I wanted to do this project was because I wanted to show the many ways in which computers complete tasks that we take for granted today. For example, in my creative project, I show people how the way in which we can easily consume music and the difference in that music is so drastically different form how music was before computer technology was introduced. I will present this information creatively by playing clips edited with computer technology and without, so people can hear the difference between how music is created today in comparison to how it was done in the past.
